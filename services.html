Waterproofing Membrane
Masonry walls are built with a damp-proof course to prevent rising damp, and the concrete in foundations needs to be damp-proofed or waterproofed with a liquid coating, basement waterproofing membrane (even under the concrete slab floor where polyethylene sheeting is commonly used), or an additive to the concrete. If you project requires Masonry walls to be waterproofed, best to go with the Pro's, not the average Joe's. Contact Pro-Form Construction and get an estimate today.
Crystalline Waterproofing
Crystalline Waterproofing allows a self-sealing. With a blend of chemicals that bonds with the available water in concrete to form insoluble crystals, these crystals grow until all pores are blocked and no water can penetrate the concrete. The crystalline formula can allow concrete to self-seal hairline cracks up 0.02 inches, even years after the original concrete pour. Contact us at Pro-Form Construction and hire the experts for your Crystalline Waterproofing.
Chemical Grout
Chemical grouting transforms granular soils into sandstone-like masses by filling the voids with a low viscosity, non-particulate grout. A sleeve port pipe is first grouted into a pre-drilled hole and the chemical grout is injected under pressure through the ports. In need of chemical grouting? Let Pro-Form Construction handle it for you.
Shotcrete
Shotcrete, gunite or sprayed concrete is concrete or mortar conveyed through a hose and pneumatically projected at high velocity onto a surface, as a construction technique. It is typically reinforced by conventional steel rods, steel mesh, or fibers. The experts at Pro-Form Construction can handle all your shotcrete projects. Contact us to schedule an appointment.
Lead Abatement
Lead abatement is an activity to reduce levels of lead, particularly in the home environment, generally to permanently eliminate lead-based paint hazards, in order to reduce or eliminate incidents of lead poisoning. Lead poisoning can be a serious hazard. Don't delay if you need this problem solved. Let us know at Pro-Form Construction, we have the solution.
Concrete Formwork
Formwork is temporary or permanent molds into which concrete or similar materials are poured. In the context of concrete construction, the falsework supports the shuttering molds. If your projects need Formwork, let the experts at Pro-Form Construction handle it for you.
